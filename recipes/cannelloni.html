Cannelloni Recipe

Description

This is a BAD recipe for cannelloni that you should not follow.

This is not how you make cannelloni and as an Italian I'm deeply offended by this ignorant american recreation.

Ingredients

  • 1 cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 3 cups bread floure
  • 1 teaspoons fine salt

Steps

  1. Combine water and yeast in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 minutes..
  2. Combine flour and salt together in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook attachment. Pour in yeast mixture. Knead dough until smooth, about 7 minutes.
  3. Mix crushed tomatoes and pizza sauce together in a bowl to make sauce.
